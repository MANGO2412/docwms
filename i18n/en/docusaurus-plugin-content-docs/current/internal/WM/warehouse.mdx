## Warehouse Module

This module serves to **register** and **manage** data about **warehouses** that each client company has, in order to **administer** them using the **WMS** system.

### General Useful View of the Module

![](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-110130.png)

<table>
    <tr>
        <th>**No**</th>
        <th>**Name**</th>
        <th>**Description**</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>**1**</th>
        <th>Total warehouses counter</th>
        <th>This section counts the total warehouses managed by the company.</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>**2**</th>
        <th>Register new warehouse button</th>
        <th>This button displays a form to register warehouses and their general information.</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>**3**</th>
        <th>Edit warehouse information button</th>
        <th>This button displays a form to edit a warehouse's general information. Each button corresponds to the record in its row that will be modified.</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <th>**4**</th>
        <th>Delete warehouse button</th>
        <th>This button deletes a warehouse's general information. Each button corresponds to the record in its row that will be deleted.</th>
    </tr>
</table>

### Register a Warehouse

<table>
    <tr>
        <th><h3>Steps</h3></th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>
            **Click** on **2** in the **general interface** to **open** a **form** for registering a new warehouse.

            Next, **enter** the required **data** about the **warehouse**.

            ![logo](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-113332.png)

            After entering the necessary information, click **ADD**. This will display the new warehouse in the main panel.

            ![logo](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-114028.png)

        </td>
    </tr>
</table>

### Edit a Warehouse

<table>
    <tr>
        <th><h3>Steps</h3></th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>
           **Click** on **3** in the **general interface** to **open** a **form** for editing warehouse fields.

            ![logo](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-114225.png)

            The warehouse **information** is now **available** for **editing**. To complete the action and save the new information, click **Edit**. The updated information will then appear in the main menu.

            ![](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-114606.png)
            
        </td>
    </tr>
</table>

### Delete a Warehouse

<table>
    <tr>
        <th><h3>Steps</h3></th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>
           **Click** on **4** in the corresponding row to **open** a small window **confirming** if you want to **delete** this warehouse.

            ![logo](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-114926.png)

           **If** you're sure about deleting the warehouse, click the **OK** button. This change will be reflected in the warehouses table.

            ![](/img/upload/Screenshot-2025-06-06-115058.png)
            
        </td>
    </tr>
</table>
